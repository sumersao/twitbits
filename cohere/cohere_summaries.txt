Researchers from the Murdoch Children's Research Institute in Melbourne have found a cure for peanut allergies. In a clinical trial in 2013, 82 per cent of participants were cured of their allergy. In a follow-up study four years later, researchers discovered 80 per cent of participants were still cured and showing no signs of allergy. Importantly, this desensitisation to peanuts persisted for up to four years after treatment.
